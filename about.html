About Us

Welcome to Prophetess Precious Outreach

Precious Ama Agyekum was born to Kwabena Agymang and Juliana Adwoa Oteng on the 4th of September, in Stuttgart, Germany. Her father Kwabena Bediako Agyemang worked with Ghana Forestry while her mother, Juliana Adwoa Oteng was a designer. Precious grew up in Accra where she had her Basic Education at Prince of Peace Academy, Hannah Preparatory and Pantang Hospital Junior High School. After her basic education, Precious continued her schooling at the Holy Trinity Cathedral School (HOTCASS), there she studied General Arts. She didn’t stop there; she proceeded to the Tertiary level of education. Apart from all these enrollments, she has also acquired quite a number of entrepreneurial skills. To mention a few, a Professional certificate in Organic Skincare, Feminine Wellness, Bead making, Household Detergents etc. She indeed has a strong passion for Entrepreneurship and Women Empowerment. Precious Ama Agyekum is a Minister of the Gospel, operating in the Prophetic Office by Divine Mandate. She is an author, a conference speaker, an entrepreneur and a philanthropist. She is the Co Senior Pastor of the World Christian Tower Ministries (WCTM), based in Ghana and Philippines, Asia. WCTM reaches out to thousands of souls through evangelistic and healing crusades as its core mandate (MISSIONS). She is the CEO of Chikan Collections, gloCutis Organic Skincare and CNA Media. She is also the Founder/President of; WCTM Women’s Prayer Network, Res-Q Women Entrepreneurship Foundation, “The Prophetess Precious Outreach “– Rescue (Children/ Youth Evangelism- Basic Schools). She is the Creator and Host/Presenter of “The Marriage Aid”. With her professionalism and spiritual background, she has helped revive many marriages and broken homes. Precious Ama Agyekum has indeed touched and is still touching many lives, both physically and spiritually. One of her key passions is, “Women Empowerment”, which makes her outstanding. She is a mother, mentor and a strong pillar of faith. Precious Ama Agyekum and Her husband, Rev Christian K. Agyekum have been married since Nov. 2009 and are parents to three wonderful children.

MISSION

The Prophetess Precious Outreach is mandated by God to RESTORE HOPE. In Africa, 41% of the population is under 15 years of age! This is very important time or stage in a child’s life, to be introduced to Jesus. In 2020 we began initiating outreach specifically for children and youth in basic schools ‘let the children come to me, and do not forbid them, for such in the kingdom of heaven – Jesus, matt 19:14’ The important of child evangelism cannot be overestimated. We can understand the importance of it when we study the scriptures where Jesus instructed his disciples to permit the children to come unto him. The Prophetess Precious Outreach is simply the type of special ministry mandated to win the heart of children/youth for Jesus. The child is saved exactly in the same way an adult is saved, by being born again. *Consider being part of this ground breaking ministry to kids/youth.

VISION

A child must repent, be baptized in the name of Jesus, and receive the Holy Ghost, exactly the same way as an adult. However, we must understand how to reach children, gain their attention, hold their special interest and win them to Jesus! Many children die the same as adult; although the average life expectancy in getting longer, yet only small percentage reach old age. Thus, out of 100,000 children, statistically, at the age of three (3) years, only 59,000 are alive at fifty (50) years of age or a little more than half. Children are more easily let to Christ than adults. They are likelier to stay converted than those converted later in life They make better Christian since there is not so much to unlearn Their lives are before them; they may devote their entire lives in the service of the Lord. An elderly person may be saved but he has nothing to offer the Lord except a wasted life. There is untold potentials in the life of a child – it staggers the imagination to think what this work would be like if everyone were converted as a child. We are mandated for children/ youth evangelism. If children are not reach with the Gospel, there is little hope for the future the main reason for so much crime and violence is that, we are raising a generation of agnostics. *Consider being part of this ground breaking ministry to Kids/Youth
